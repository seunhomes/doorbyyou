What's covered, and what isn't

This warranty applies to the original product as delivered, installed according to our published guidelines, and used in a normal residential setting. It begins on the delivery date.

Covered

  • Manufacturing and material defects in the slab, frame and weather seal
  • Insulated-glass seal failure, fogging and stress cracks not caused by impact
  • Finish peeling, blistering or excessive fade within the finish term
  • Mechanical failure of locks, hinges and operating hardware under normal use

Not covered

  • Damage from improper installation, alteration or non-approved hardware
  • Impact, accident, misuse, vandalism or acts of nature
  • Normal wear, surface scratches and minor finish variation over time
  • Doors finished or painted by a third party after delivery
  • Labor for removal or reinstallation

How long it lasts

Slab, frame and sealed-glass coverage runs for the life of the original purchaser's ownership and transfers once to a subsequent owner. Finish and hardware coverage run ten years from delivery. All terms are subject to the full written warranty provided with your order.
